Welcome to the Chicago, IL 60607 Apartment

About this home Stunning 3Bed/2.5Bath corner condo in the gorgeous, newer construction 1400 W Monroe Residences! Distinctly upgraded unit includes hardwood floors throughout, custom designed kitchen by Mojo Stumer with 3 cm quartz countertops, beautiful custom cabinetry, custom built-in Thermador appliances & Bosch dishwasher, sleek pendants & undercabinet kitchen lighting. Additional features include custom recessed lighting, Lutron Caseta Wireless Lighting Technology package with thermostat controls, RH dining light fixture, oversized black framed windows with custom remote shades in living/dining area, and private covered weather resistant balcony to enjoy grilling or afternoon sunsets. Primary suite features custom lighting, custom window treatments, expertly-designed dressing room with California closets and concealed storage for a luxury look as well as floating double vanity in primary bathroom with 3 cm quartz countertops, dual illuminated frameless wall mounted medicine cabinets, Kallista rain shower head with separate shower spray, and Kallista faucets. Ideal location on tree-lined street walkable to Skinner Park, right across from West Loop 569 Dog Park, Whitney Young Magnet High School, and Michelle Obama Athletic Field Park & Tennis Courts. Near Mariano's, Target, Whole Foods, WNDR Museum, Chicago Children's Theatre, highways, all Chicago's best restaurants/nightlife, and in Skinner School District.
