About this home
Incredible, modern home in highly desirable Azure neighborhood of The Ridges. Situated on a huge, near-acre lot w/ utmost privacy & amazing mountain views, this home has style, finish and build qualities that are second to none. Fantastic floor plan with dual primary suites-one up, one down. Both suites have gorgeous spa-quality baths & spectacular walk-in closets. The chef’s dream kitchen is loaded with Wolf & Sub-Zero appliances, double islands, breakfast counter, walk-in pantry. Multiple pocketing doors create ultimate indoor/outdoor vibe and lead to oasis-like backyard. Enormous pool, chic cabana, full outdoor kitchen & putting green transform the yard into the ultimate private resort. Great room features a showcase wine cellar & large bar—perfect for entertaining. Upstairs game room converts into movie theater. Large driveway secured w/ powered gate. Oversized secondary bedrooms have en suite bath & walk-in closets. Separate office. Recently updated whole-home Crestron A/V system.
