About this home
Step into a world of warmth and light in this exquisite 3 bedroom, 3 bathroom home, offering breathtaking views of Central Park. The kitchen is a model of modern luxury, featuring a marble island, countertops, and equipped with premium Viking and Bosch appliances. The crown jewel of this home is the delightful private terrace. Here, you can enjoy the beauty of Central Park and embrace indoor/outdoor living for most of the year, creating unforgettable memories under the open sky. The west-facing primary bedroom is a serene retreat, complete with an en-suite bath that includes both a shower and soaking tub. It's a space where relaxation meets luxury, complemented by an extensive wall of built-in closets and an additional custom walk-in closet.
