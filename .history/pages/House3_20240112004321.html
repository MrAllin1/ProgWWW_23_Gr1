About this home

Indulge in the epitome of opulence at this newly built residence in Mar Vista, a flawless masterpiece of luxury living! Meticulously crafted, this four-bedroom, four-bath marvel seamlessly blends modern aesthetics with purposeful functionality. Upon entry, be welcomed by the grandeur of a den adorned with barn doors and sliding doors leading to the courtyard. The guest bedroom, complemented by a full bath, enhances the inviting ambiance. The chef-inspired kitchen commands attention, overseeing the expansive great room and dining area. Revel in the highlights of an island with bar seating, a walk-in pantry, and a home management area boasting a built-in desk. Effortless indoor-outdoor entertaining is achieved with sliding doors in the formal dining area and an adjacent morning room. The great room, featuring a captivating fireplace, unfolds to the courtyard, creating an idyllic space to unwind. Ascend the stairs to an upper level revealing a loft, two additional secondary bedrooms with a shared bath, a laundry room with a sink, and a deck. The grand suite is a haven of tranquility, showcasing a spa-like bath with a freestanding soaking tub, a walk-in shower, dual sink vanity, linen closet, and dual walk-in closets. This residence transcends mere living; it offers a curated experience tailored to the most discerning standards of modern luxury!
